About Me

Joel Murtaugh comes from Southeast Kentucky and is currently located in Louisville, Kentucky.
As a bassist, he has had experience playing jazz, theater, rock, pop, and improvisational styles.
He has been an active teacher for the past four years and has been lucky to have such a diverse and engaging studio during this time.
His compositions draw upon the rich history of American music while aiming for something personal and unique.
