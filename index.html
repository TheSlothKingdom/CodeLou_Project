About me

Joel Murtaugh comes from Southeast Kentucky and is currently located in Louisville, Kentucky. As a bassist he has had expreience playing jazz, theatre, rock, pop, and improvasational styles. His compositions draw upon the rich history of American music while aiming for something personal and unique. You can get into contact with Joel here!
